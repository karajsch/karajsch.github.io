    I am a Web Designer creating user-focused e-commerce experiences that are both visually engaging and effective.

    As a web designer with 3+ years of experience, I focus on crafting digital experiences that are as functional as they are visually engaging. I work with cross-functional teams to simplify complex content, ensure accessibility, and stay ahead of UX/UI trends. With a solid foundation in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I use A/B testing to refine performance and design custom graphics in Photoshop and Illustrator to elevate the user experience. My goal is always to create websites that are seamless, user-centered, and aligned with brand standards.

    My background in art and print has shaped the way I approach my work. These experiences inform everything I do now, grounding me in a design philosophy that values both aesthetics and functionality, and ensures that every project feels considered and cohesive.
