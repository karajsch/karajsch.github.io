About

Hello! I'm a full stack developer based in Philadelphia. In February of 2021, I graduated from University of Pennsylvania LPS Coding Bootcamp certificate program. I'm obsessed with all things art and design and love playing with modular synthesizers, reading sci-fi novels, collecting houseplants, 35mm photography, listening to science podcasts, and adding to my ever growing stash of artist books/zines/graphic novels. I have a BFA in Film/Cinema Studies from Pratt Institute and an MFA in Digital & Interdisciplinary Practice from City University of New York.

The intersection of code, design, and environmentalism is an endless source of fascination for me. Previous to becoming a front end developer, I worked in commercial printing, the art world, and academia. In learning more about coding, I hope to interact with the technology that surrounds me in a more meaningful way.

Portfolio

A few select projects... more to come soon!

Fridge For All

HTML, CSS/Bootstrap, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB, Axios, React, Heroku

Fridge For All is an application made in collaboration with Meredith Jones and Luke Martin to help community fridges in Philadelphia. Did you know there are over 14 community fridges in Philadelphia? Part of the challenge of supporting a community fridge is knowing what is most needed in advance of dropping off provisions. Fridge For All aims to tackle this unique issue by creating an interactive database where users can see what's most urgent ahead of time, and plan accordingly.

User Directory

HTML, CSS/Bootstrap, Handlebars, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, React

This simple application is an employee directory app populated with randomized users via Random User Generator. User Directory uses React to create data that's easily searchable and sortable, so it's easy to find a particular person or a group with commonalities by just clicking!

Budget Tracker

HTML, CSS/Bootstrap, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB, Mongo, Heroku

Budget Tracker is a progressive web application that saves your total balance regardless of whether you have an internet connection. Budget Tracker uses an Express powered server and IndexedDB (a JavaScript-based object-oriented database) to safely store your entries. You can add add funds or subtract from the balance as you travel and be sure that your information will be stored and the right total is always shown.

Dungeon Master Manager

HTML, CSS/Bootstrap, Handlebars, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, MySql, Heroku, Passport/Bcrypt

Dungeon Master Manager (DMM) is a full stack application powered by Node.js and Express.js made in collaboration with John Hauth and Brandon Grayson. You can create a unique profile to keep your Dungeons & Dragons adventures organized. As a dungeon master, you need to keep track of all the characters in your game and keep track of where you are in the story. Using DMM, you can go into your profile and select which saved game you want to look up. You can see all of the characters that are involved within the game and even add new characters! Unleash your inner master and take control of your dungeon!

Eat-Da-Burger

HTML, CSS/Bootstrap, Handlebars, JavaScript, Node.js, Express.js, MySql, Heroku

Eat-Da-Burger is a burger logging app powered by MySQL, Node, Express, and Handlebars! Burgers appear in the left area from you to choose from, or you can add as many new burgers as you want by typing your perfect burger in the text area then clicking the "add burger" button. Click the "devour it!" button to devour it, then it will be logged in the right area. This application uses Node and MySQL to query and route data while Handlebars generates the HTML. All burgers will be saved on a database on a server, whether eaten or not.

Plant Scout

HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, GoogleFonts, Trefle API, Open Weather API,

Plant Scout is an app made in collaboration with Gregory Leighton and Tamira Bell which encourages people to go outside and spend time in nature. In Plant Scout, the user can enter their location and get weather data in the form of temperature, humidity, UV index, visibility, as well as sunrise and sunset via Open Weather Map API. The user can also search for plants using any keyword they choose, and will be presented with a list of every plant from Trefle API that matches that keyword as well as a photo of the plant, the scientific name, and family name.

Day Planner
HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, GoogleFonts

A straightforward scheduler with hour-long blocks of time between 9 am through 5pm. The current date and local time is displayed at the top. The 9-5 hour blocks are green if the block is upcoming, red if it is that hour presently, and gray if the hour has already passed. Notes for each block can be alied by clicking the appropriate block and then hitting the "Save" button to the right!

Password Generator
HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, GoogleFonts

A functional password generating application that creates a completely random password based on whether the user prefers lower or uppercase, numbers, and/or special characters. The user is prompted to specify which criteria they prefer and as long as their preferred amount of characters is between 8 and 128, the generator will create a unique password. If the user enters no criteria, they will not be able to use the generator.
