    I am a Web Designer creating user-focused ecommerce experiences that are both visually engaging and effective.

    After gaining experience in print shops, photography labs, and teaching Art & Visual Perception and 2D Imaging, I transitioned into ecommerce by completing a 12-week full-stack web development bootcamp, which provided the technical foundation for my new career. With 3 years of web design experience, I now specialize in creating user-centered digital experiences by collaborating with diverse teams, simplifying complex content, ensuring accessibility, and staying ahead of UX/UI trends. Proficient in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I use A/B testing to optimize performance and design custom graphics with Photoshop and Illustrator to enhance the user experience. This blend of creativity and technical expertise allows me to craft seamless, functional, and visually compelling websites.
